About Anish

Greetings! I'm Anish Lopchan, hailing from the picturesque town of Darjeeling, nestled amidst the majestic Himalayas. It's a place that has shaped my perspective, fostering a deep appreciation for nature's beauty and tranquility. In 2019, I embarked on my educational journey at Darjeeling Polytechnic Institute, where I pursued a diploma in Computer Science. It was a period of rigorous learning and hands-on experiences that laid the foundation for my career in technology.

Following my diploma, I pursued my passion for computer science further and enrolled at Ideal Institute of Engineering. In 2022, I proudly graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, equipped with a diverse skill set and a thirst for knowledge. My time at Ideal Institute was transformative, allowing me to delve deeper into various aspects of computer science, from programming to data structures, and beyond. It was a fulfilling journey that prepared me to take on the challenges of the tech industry with confidence.

Beyond academics, I find solace in pursuing my hobbies, which include photography, hiking, and playing musical instruments. Photography allows me to capture the beauty of my surroundings, while hiking rejuvenates my spirit and connects me with nature. Music is another passion of mine, providing an outlet for creativity and self-expression. With a blend of academic achievements and personal interests, I'm eager to embark on the next chapter of my journey, ready to contribute meaningfully to the world of technology and beyond.
